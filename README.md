### Game details
Survive as long as you can! 

### How to play
* WASD: Move
* Left Click: Attack
* Right Click: Block
* Space: Dash
  
Blocking consumes 1 stamina, if your stamina reaches 0 you lose the ability to block until you restore at least 1 stamina back, your attack speed becomes impaired too. 
Blocking an attack quickly after an enemy attacks, counts as a parry. Doing a parry recovers 1 stamina and staggers the enemy for longer.
